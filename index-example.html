The influence of my childhood on me as
a designer

My name is Clíona Horgan. I am a critical thinker with a willingness to innovate and incorporate new ideas into my work. I constantly look for inspiration in the world around me and draw from my own life experiences. My work is user centered leading to a unique outcome for every project I undertake. Nostalgic memories heighten my creativity, to create meaningful design solutions. My childhood has had a big impact on the designer I am today. As a child I always enjoyed drawing, face painting and playing games which were highly imaginative. This has made me the creative thinker and problem solver I am today. Perhaps this is why childhood has been described as the best education.
