Thrive

Disciplines: UI/UX, motion
Client: HSE
Date: May 2019

Thrive is an organisation that supports adults who had cancer as children. There is currently a lack of support for adults who had cancer as children in Ireland. With more and more children surviving cancer the need for aftercare is apparent. Thrive aims to fill this gap in support. The brand is built around the idea of supporting people in their journey through life after cancer. This is reflected in the identity with the use of the lines/hills to represent this journey. The use of illustration and bright colours aims to create a welcoming atmosphere. I would like to thank CanCare4living and Canteen Ireland for their support they gave me throughout this project.
