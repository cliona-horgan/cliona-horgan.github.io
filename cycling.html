Respect Cyclists
For this project we were each given a word, mine being cyclist. We were either for or against the word, we had to make a motion piece based on this. I was for cyclists, as a result I came up with a respect cyclists campaign.

My thinking

My inspiration for this project was my love of cycling from a young age. I used this as inspiration for my project to create a Respect Cyclists campaign. I wanted it to have an impact on everyone, so I used language such as “your Mom, your Dad” in order to get people to stop and think about how they treat cyclists.
