Almost
Lost

Disciplines: Print, editorial, typography
Award: ISTD
Date: January 2019

This publication contains a collection of Irish Folklore collected by the Irish folklore commission in the 1930s. It focuses on the loss of history, culture and beliefs we could have faced were it not for the work of the Irish Folklore Commission. Almost Lost is aimed at Irish people at home and among the Irish diaspora. It aims to be a tangible way of connecting with Irish culture. The Irish theme is reflected in the abstracted typographic landscapes displayed throughout the publication. This project recieved an award by the International Society of Typographic designers.
