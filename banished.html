Banished

Disciplines: Print, editorial, typography
Date: October 2018

Banished is an award winning opera by Stephen McNeff, it tells the story of female prisioners who were transported from Ireland to Australia at the end of the 18th century. The brutality the women faced on the journey could not be ignored throughout the opera. As a result I created a poster which reflected the brutality and oppresion the women faced. This visual style is continued through to the and tickets.
