Bord Bia

Disciplines: Branding, spatial, Motion
Date: September 2017
Collaborator: Cian Pawle-Bates

The Thinking House is both a built and globally connected virtual environment within Bord Bia that ensures the branding and innovation chains across the food and drink industry in Ireland will start with the consumer and end with the consumer. The challenge was to use the exsisting windows to showcase the work the Thinking House does. We took the approach of “going beneath the surface” came from a quote from the Thinking House video “It’s not just about the surface its about going deep to understand what the consumers really feel.” We formed our visual language around this concept of digging deep and researching in order to understand consumers and as a result of this research helping businesses grow.
