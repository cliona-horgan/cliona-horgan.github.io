Separate
& Distinct

Disciplines: Print, Editorial
Date: May 2017

Separate and Distinct is a self authored exhibition catalogue based on the struggle for Irish National identity 1916-1937. This struggle is visually represented through the use of distressed typography and graphic forms. Handwritten notes from the period give a personable feel which aims to appeal to the audiences emotions.
